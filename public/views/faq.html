FAQ

Wanting to buy a hammock? Here is how to get started?

Our goal is to make our hammocks as hassle free as possible. All of our hammocks come with end-straps for extra strength and durability so our customers never have to worry about their knots coming undone. We also provide tree sleeves with pre-tied knots to protect from fray and provide even extra durability. Our customer never have to tie their own knots! Each hammock also comes with a set of aluminum wire-gate carabiners that work with our suspension system. All you need to do now is choose your of hammock.

How do I wash my Make Tracks hammock?

You can machine wash or hand wash your hammock. (NOTE: Do not put the hammock in the dryer! Air dry only!)

Machine Wash: First, remove the carabiners, then wash on cool in a front load washer on the delicate cycle seting. Use a mild detergent. Do not untie any knots!

Hand Wash: We recommend using a cloth with soap or mild detergent.

How do I care for my Make Tracks hammock?

Safety: In set up, insure that your straps are securely wrapped and the carabiners are closed. Ease into your hammock slowly to make sure it hangs securely.

UV Damage: UV damage can make the hammock fade in color and weaken the fabric which leads to tearing. We recommend when the hammock is not in use; pack it in its sack and store somewhere dry and cool.

Does the hammock come with straps?

Our hammock package comes with everything you need for a great hammock experience. We use end-straps instead of knoted rope to attach to our carabiners for their reliability and strength. The package also comes with tree-sleeves, which consists a pre-knoted rope, encased by a nylon material to protect the rope from fraying.

Will I need straps to use the hammock?

No, you do not need any extra straps than what is provided in the hammock package.
